🧹 RemoveAI

AI-text & citation checker for arXiv submissions

Finds chat-assistant artifacts and hallucinated references in .tex / .bib / .bbl / .pdf / .txt files using key phrases, regular expressions and cross-checks against Semantic Scholar, CrossRef and OpenAlex (which also index arXiv). No generative AI is used anywhere. Your files never leave the browser — only citation titles/DOIs are sent to the public APIs for verification.
